Flip-Flop Machine Simulator

This web page simulates a particular machine built from DEC Laboratory Digital Modules.

You can wire and simulate your own machine, and view and copy machines that others have built.

This simulator simulates the following modules:

  • Type 110 Diode Gate module, consisting of two diode gates and two clamped load terminals.
  • Type 201 Flip-Flop module, consisting of one flip-flop, two inverters, and three ground terminals. (See a picture at the Computer History Museum.)
  • Type 401 Clock module.
  • Type 5402 Clock module.
  • Type 602 Pulse Amplifier module, consisting of two pulse amplifiers, two inverters, and three grounds. (See a picture of the similar of a Type 3602 Pulse Amplifier at the Computer History Museum.)
